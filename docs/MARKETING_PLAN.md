# AU Track — Marketing Plan

## Target Audience

Anna University undergraduate and postgraduate students (B.E, B.Tech, M.E, M.Tech) across all branches — primarily 2nd to 4th year students who actively track their academic performance.

## Positioning

"The privacy-first academic companion built specifically for Anna University students. Upload your grade screenshot, get instant CGPA, track arrears, and never miss a university notification."

## Phase 1: First 10 Users (Weeks 1–2)

### Strategy: Direct Outreach
Goal: Get 10 active users who provide feedback on core features.

### Channels
1. **Personal network** — Classmates, friends, study groups (5 users)
2. **WhatsApp college groups** — Share with own batch and junior batches (3 users)
3. **Telegram Anna University groups** — Post in active AU student Telegram channels (2 users)

### Message Template (WhatsApp/Telegram)
```
Hey! I built an app called AU Track specifically for Anna University students.

✅ Upload your grade screenshot → grades extracted automatically (OCR)
✅ Instant GPA/CGPA calculation
✅ Arrear tracking
✅ University notifications in one place
✅ 100% private — screenshots processed in your browser, never uploaded

It's free. Try it out: [link]

Would love your feedback! 🙏
```

### Feedback Questions
1. What was your first impression when you opened the app?
2. Did the OCR upload work for your screenshot? Was it accurate?
3. Was the GPA calculation clear and easy to understand?
4. What's one feature you wish the app had?
5. Would you recommend this to a classmate? Why or why not?
6. Did you notice the privacy notice? Did it make you feel more comfortable?
7. Was anything confusing or hard to find?
8. How likely are you to use this next semester when results come out?

### Success Criteria
- 10 registered users
- At least 5 complete onboarding
- At least 3 use OCR upload
- 5+ feedback responses collected

## Phase 2: First 100 Users (Weeks 3–6)

### Strategy: Community + Content
Goal: Reach 100 registered users through community engagement and content marketing.

### Channels

#### 1. Instagram Reels (Primary)
- **Content:** Screen recordings of OCR upload, GPA calculation, arrear tracking
- **Hook:** "Stop calculating CGPA manually. Do this instead 👇"
- **Frequency:** 3 reels per week
- **Hashtags:** #annauniversity #cgpa #annauniversityupdates #engineering #collegelife #auexam

#### 2. WhatsApp Groups (Scale)
- Ask Phase 1 users to share with their class groups
- Create a referral loop (see below)
- Post in AU-specific WhatsApp groups (with permission)

#### 3. Telegram Channels
- Post in Anna University notification Telegram channels
- Share notification feature as value proposition
- "Get AU notifications in one place + track your CGPA"

#### 4. Reddit / Quora
- Answer questions on r/annauniversity about CGPA calculation
- Mention AU Track as a tool in relevant Quora answers
- Don't spam — provide value first, mention app naturally

#### 5. College-specific Instagram Pages
- Reach out to college meme pages and info pages
- Offer free shoutout exchange or pay for small promotions
- Target: 5 college pages, 2k–10k followers each

### Content Calendar (Weekly)

| Day | Content | Channel |
|-----|---------|---------|
| Monday | Feature highlight reel | Instagram |
| Wednesday | Notification update + app mention | Telegram + WhatsApp |
| Friday | Student testimonial or use case | Instagram |
| Saturday | Quora/Reddit answer with app mention | Quora/Reddit |

### Message Template (Instagram Bio/Link)
```
AU Track 🎓
Track your CGPA, arrears & AU notifications
📸 OCR grade upload | 📊 GPA calculator
🔗 [link]
Not affiliated with Anna University
```

### Success Criteria
- 100 registered users
- 40+ weekly active users
- 20+ OCR uploads per week
- 4.0+ average feedback rating
- 10+ organic shares by users

## Referral Idea

### "Track Together" Referral Program
- Each user gets a unique referral link: `au-track.com/?ref=USERNAME`
- When a referred user completes onboarding, both get a "Track Together" badge on their dashboard
- Milestone rewards: Refer 3 friends → "Ambassador" badge + priority feature requests
- Leaderboard: "Top Referrers" (optional, Phase 4)

### Implementation
- Track referral source via URL parameter on signup
- Store `referred_by` in profiles table
- Display badge on dashboard
- No monetary incentives — keep it community-driven

## Marketing Principles

1. **Privacy is a feature** — Lead with "screenshots never leave your browser"
2. **Student-to-student** — Marketing is peer recommendations, not corporate ads
3. **Show, don't tell** — Screen recordings > text descriptions
4. **Be where students are** — WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram (not LinkedIn)
5. **Non-affiliation is non-negotiable** — Always include disclaimer in promotional content
6. **Free forever** — No paywall, no premium tier for core features
